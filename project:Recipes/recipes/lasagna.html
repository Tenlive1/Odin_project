Lasagna Recipes

Desription

an italian dish made up of layers of wide pasta sheets, tomato sauce, meat or vegetables

Ingredients

  • pasta sheets
  • ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. grab a long rectangle bowl and put a layer of pasta sheets in that rectangle bowls
  2. put a layer of tomato sauce and ground beef and then another pasta sheets
  3. put a layer of tomato sauce and cheese and a layer of pasta sheets
    4. put a layer of cheese and bake it
